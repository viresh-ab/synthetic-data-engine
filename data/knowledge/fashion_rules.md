# Fashion Domain Rules (RAG Source)

These rules describe realistic fashion behavior patterns and preferences.
They are used to ground LLM-generated text and numeric constraints.

## General Fashion Behavior
- Fashion preferences vary by age, lifestyle, and occupation.
- Comfort and fit are as important as style for most consumers.
- Trends influence interest, but not everyone follows them strictly.

## Age-Based Tendencies
- Teenagers and college students are more trend-driven and experimental.
- Working professionals prefer versatile, comfortable, and practical clothing.
- Older consumers prioritize quality, durability, and brand trust.

## Shopping Channels
- Younger consumers prefer online shopping due to convenience and variety.
- Offline shopping is preferred for formal wear and premium clothing.
- Discounts and seasonal sales strongly influence purchase decisions.

## Brand & Style Preferences
- Brand loyalty increases with age and income.
- Budget-conscious consumers balance price with quality.
- Fashion enthusiasts actively follow social media, influencers, and trends.

## Tone Guidance for Text Generation
- Responses should sound natural and personal.
- Avoid generic marketing language.
- Vary sentence length and phrasing.
- Do not repeat exact expressions across rows.
